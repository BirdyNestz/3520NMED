My Interpretaion:

A community can share the same set of beliefs, and at the same time they can still be very different based on their understanding and interpretation of the concept of beliefs. For example, just because I cannot speak the fluent Blackfoot language, does not mean I am not Piikani First Nations. Everyone is brought up differently, whether or not if that is because how you are raised and influenced by parents and guardians, or how they you are treated within society because of how you look, talk and lineage. I understand there should be requirements in a culture for example, elders who want to teach the younger generation should understand the history of the Blackfoot culture and the traditional practices, to properly pass on the traditions.
