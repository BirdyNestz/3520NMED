Liberal Education 2000 Identity and Liberal Ed.

Identity and Liberal Education is an important course because it challenges students to ask deeper questions of once's self and what Identity means to an individual. What is identity and how are we shaped from it? Taking on these concepts of identity allow a student to have the ability to develop their own self-discovery.
